The LOOMIA LAB program

Each LEL is slightly customized to properly fit into your supply chain and manufacturing process. The LOOMIA Lab program takes you from concept to a pilot production run. We work with our customers to go from lab prototype, to pilot production, to scale production. Our LEL has been designed for easy integration, meaning that your product maintains your existing supply chain at scale.

Phase 1: CONCEPT

- Concept Refinement
- Market and User Research
- Use Case discovery
- LEL Sampling
- Functionality Specs

Phase 2: Design & Engineering

- Functional prototype that can be used for marketing, POC, or internal buy-in.
- User interviews and user testing
- Product Design & Engineering Iterations
-LEL customization

Phase 3: DFM Refinement

- Functionality Refinement
- DFM Iterations
- Safety and performance tests with partner organizations
- Assembly guide & tech pack development
- Production Tests

Phase 4: Pilot Production

- Pilot LEL Production
- Pilot assembly ( at your manufacturing facility or our partner assembly houses)
- Certifications as needed (FCC,CE, etc.)
- Scalability plan
- Marketing assistance
