Loomia design and manufactures soft circuit systems (e-textiles) that can be produced at scale. Our technology can add functionality to textiles in automotive, consumer apparel and industrial environments.

About our products

Our products & services

Loomia works with a range of companies who produce soft good products including automotive, wearables, personal protective equipment and more.

We specialize in producing a patented soft circuit system called the LOOMIA Electronic Layer, or LEL for short. The LEL is an e-textile that is designed to integrate into textile products. These circuits can light, sense and heat.

We customize the size, shape and functionality of our LEL for your product through the LOOMIA lab program, where we work with you from lab prototype to scale production.
